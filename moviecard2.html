Storyline

"Tumbbad" tells the story of a man named Vinayak Rao who seeks a hidden treasure in a cursed village, Tumbbad, where the goddess of prosperity's cursed firstborn son, Hastar, resides. Hastar is greedy for gold and food, and his presence is a source of both riches and curses. Vinayak's greed leads him to take risks and make deals with Hastar, ultimately leading to a terrifying and morally complex climax.
